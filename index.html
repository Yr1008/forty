The Challenge

Chitrapotli had one extraordinary asset: world-class traditional Pichwai art. What it lacked was everything else: a digital identity, a distribution framework, and a path to the modern collector. The brief was clear: transform a local craft into a global brand.

The Solution

We treated this as an integrated design and marketplace build. Visual storytelling came first: a brand identity and professional creative assets that elevated perceived value from the first scroll.

Then, a dual-path commerce strategy:

  • Global Flagship: A Shopify storefront optimized for conversion and international logistics.
  • Marketplace Leadership: End-to-end Amazon India management, from SEO-optimized listings and professional mockups to high-performance ad campaigns.

By streamlining discovery-to-fulfillment across owned and marketplace channels, the brand could scale globally from day one.

The Result

  • Market Visibility: 100 million+ cross-platform impressions annually, establishing deep brand awareness.
  • Momentum: 21,000% growth from a standing start.
  • Global Footprint: Active presence across 6 countries, transforming a local art form into an international brand.

The Challenge

Integrating technology into personal style is a delicate balance. The objective was to position the Ray-Ban × Meta collection as an essential lifestyle accessory, not a piece of hardware. The creative strategy had to favor human utility over technical specifications.

The Solution

We partnered with influencers to deliver a lifestyle-first narrative. No feature lists, no spec sheets. Instead, native storytelling that showcased the glasses in the natural rhythm of daily life: indoors, outdoors, in motion.

By prioritizing each creator’s authentic perspective, we demonstrated the product’s value within the context of their world. The result: content that felt native to the feed while subtly reinforcing intuitive design.

The Result

  • Market Awareness: 126k+ views, establishing significant digital presence for the product launch.
  • Native Engagement: Creative that earned organic placement by prioritizing lifestyle over hardware.
  • Market Perception: Successfully shifted the narrative from wearable tech to essential fashion accessory.

Mashreq Bank

Turning a corporate sustainability initiative into a compelling human story. Documentary-style content that outperformed industry benchmarks by 2×.

5.4% Engagement Rate 2× Industry Average
The Impact
0 Engagement Rate
0 Industry Average

The Challenge

Corporate sustainability initiatives often struggle to communicate impact with authenticity. For Mashreq Bank’s Climb2Change initiative, the challenge was to document an environmental trek in a way that felt inspiring and human, without the sterile tone of traditional corporate media.

The Solution

We treated the initiative as a human-interest story. Using a documentary-style approach, our team followed volunteers and creators as they cleared mountain routes, capturing raw effort and the genuine exchange between team members and locals.

By centering the lived experience of participants, we let the bank’s values speak through action rather than corporate messaging.

The Result

  • Community Resonance: 5.4% engagement-to-view rate, more than double the industry average for corporate CSR content.
  • High-Value Advocacy: Exceptional repost and share volume, signaling deeply “save-worthy” content aligned with audience values.
  • Narrative Integrity: Elevated the brand’s sustainability profile through authentic, human-centered storytelling.
  • Strategic Reach: Transformed a CSR initiative into a compelling narrative that built deep affinity with a global audience.
